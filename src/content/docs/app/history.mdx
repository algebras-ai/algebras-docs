## View history
1. Open History.
2. See recent runs with status, language, and date.
3. Click a row for details.

## Filter and search
- Filter by date range, status, language, or user.
- Search by text, filename, or job ID.

## Actions
- Re-open a run to refine and re-run.
- Download outputs (text, subtitles, audio) if available.
- Export CSV for reporting when provided.

## Tips
- Use filters to locate large batches quickly.
- Keep runs tied to projects for easier tracking.
