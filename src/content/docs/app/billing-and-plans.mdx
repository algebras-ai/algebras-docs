## Overview

Plans are scoped to organizations. Each organization has its own subscription, allowing you to manage billing separately for different teams or projects.

## Available Plans

### Text Standard

**Monthly**: $15/month | **Annual**: $12.50/month ($150/year)

- 15,000 words per month included
- Manual text/document input
- Basic translation features
- Overages billed per unit when quota is exceeded

**Best for**: Individuals and small teams getting started with translation.

### Text Pro

**Monthly**: $45/month | **Annual**: $37.50/month ($450/year)

- All Text Standard features, plus:
- 45,000 words per month included
- **Glossaries**: Create and use term glossaries
- **API access**: Integrate translations via REST API
- Extended document formats
- Overages billed per unit when quota is exceeded

**Best for**: Growing teams needing glossaries and API integration.

### Premium Localization

**Monthly**: $75/month | **Annual**: $62.50/month ($750/year)

- All Text Pro features, plus:
- 75,000 words per month included
- Personal account manager
- Custom integrations
- Priority support
- Overages billed per unit when quota is exceeded

**Best for**: Large organizations with complex localization needs.

## Managing Your Subscription

### Upgrading or Downgrading

1. Navigate to **Plans** (under Organization in the sidebar, Owner access only).
2. Review available plans and their features.
3. Select **Monthly** or **Annual** billing frequency.
4. Click **Get started** or **Upgrade** on your desired plan.
5. Complete the checkout process via Stripe.
6. Your new plan activates immediately or at the start of the next billing cycle (depending on timing).

### Viewing Current Plan

1. Go to **Plans** or **Billing** section.
2. Your current plan is highlighted and marked as "Current plan".
3. View your monthly/annual billing frequency.
4. Check your usage against your monthly quota.

## Usage and Quotas

- **Word limits**: Each plan includes a monthly word quota for translations.
- **Overage billing**: Usage beyond your quota is billed on a metered basis.
- **Monitoring**: Track your usage in the dashboard to avoid unexpected charges.
- **Reset period**: Quotas reset monthly based on your billing cycle.

## Billing Frequency

Choose between:
- **Monthly billing**: Pay month-to-month with flexibility
- **Annual billing**: Save ~17% with yearly subscription

You can change billing frequency when upgrading or renewing.

## Payment Methods

- Payment is handled securely through Stripe.
- Supported payment methods include major credit cards.
- Update your payment method anytime in the billing settings.
- Failed payments may suspend service until resolved.

## Invoices

- **Access**: View and download invoices from the Billing section.
- **Format**: Invoices are available as PDFs.
- **History**: All past invoices are retained for your records.
- **Email notifications**: Receive email confirmations for payments and invoices.

## Organization-Level Billing

- Each organization maintains its own subscription and billing.
- Switch organizations to view their respective billing information.
- Team members with Owner role can manage billing for their organization.
- Separate billing allows different teams to use different plans as needed.

## Tips

1. **Monitor usage**: Regularly check your usage dashboard to stay within quota or plan ahead for upgrades.
2. **Annual savings**: Choose annual billing for better value if you're committed long-term.
3. **Plan ahead**: Upgrade before hitting quota limits to avoid overage charges.
4. **Team organization**: Create separate organizations with different plans for different use cases.
5. **Contact support**: Reach out if you need custom plans or have billing questions.
