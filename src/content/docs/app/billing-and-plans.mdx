## Plans and limits
- **Tiers**: choose a plan that fits your volume.
- **Quotas**: monthly credits/usage caps per plan.
- **Overage**: usage beyond quota may bill per unit (if enabled).

## Manage subscription
1. Open Billing.
2. Select a plan: upgrade/downgrade.
3. Confirm payment method.
4. Changes apply next cycle or immediately (pro-rated when applicable).

## Invoices and payment
- View invoice history and download PDFs.
- Update payment method and billing details.
- Handle failed payments promptly to avoid suspension.

## Tips
- Monitor usage; adjust plan before hitting caps.
- Use org-level billing for teams.
