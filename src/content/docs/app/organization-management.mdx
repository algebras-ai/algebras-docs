## Create and switch organizations
1. Open Organization menu.
2. Create new org or switch via selector.

## Invite and manage members
1. Go to Members.
2. Invite by email.
3. Assign a role.
4. Revoke or change roles anytime.

## Roles (typical)
- **Owner**: billing, roles, settings, all data.
- **Admin**: manage members, resources, most settings.
- **Member**: create/use resources.
- **Viewer**: read-only access.

## Best practices
- Use least-privilege roles.
- Separate personal and team orgs.
- Review membership regularly.
