## Edit and manage
- Add, edit, or delete terms anytime.
- Import/export CSV for bulk updates.
- Version by duplicating a glossary before major edits.

## AI Extraction (from text/files)
1. Create or open a glossary.
2. Choose AI Extraction.
3. Provide sample text or upload content.
4. Review suggested terms; accept or adjust mappings.
5. Save.

## Use during translation
1. In Playground, attach a glossary.
2. Run translation; terms are enforced.
3. If a term is missed, add/update it and re-run.

## Tips
- Keep terms short, unambiguous, and product-specific.
- Prefer singular base forms; add inflections only when necessary.
