---

## What you can do
- **Subtitle translation**: generate translated captions.
- **Dubbing**: synthesize translated audio tracks.
- **Languages**: select one or multiple targets.
- **Export**: SRT/VTT, MP3/WAV, packaged ZIP.

## Workflow
1. Go to Video Localization.
2. Upload a video or select from library.
3. Choose target languages.
4. Pick output: subtitles, dubbing, or both.
5. Optional: attach a glossary and set tone.
6. Start processing; wait for completion.
7. Review previews; accept or re-run.
8. Download subtitles/audio or full package.

## Languages
- Common: en, es, fr, de, it, pt, ja, ko, zh, ru, ar, hi.
- Availability may expand; check UI for the latest list.

## Tips
- For names/brands, use a glossary.
- Ensure source audio is clear for better alignment.
